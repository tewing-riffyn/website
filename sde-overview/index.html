The Riffyn SDE supports every step of discovery & development

 
 
 

Computer-aided design for science

1. VISUAL PROCESS DESIGN

With Riffyn's CAD-like user interface for experimental process design, you can define all material and sample handling steps, all parameters and measured properties, calculations, and specification limits.


2. VERSIONING

Riffyn retains every version of process and experiment designs, making it possible to visualize changes across time and assess their impact on experimental outputs.


3. BUILT-IN SCIENTIFIC KNOWLEDGE

Riffyn uses standard and customizable ontologies for experimental data to ensure consistency and comparability of data.

Execute flawless experiments

1. DESIGN OF EXPERIMENTS / QBD

Riffyn's experiment engine allows you to design multi-parameter experiments and track complex sample flows through your processing steps.


2. CONTEXTUALIZED DATA ACQUISITION

Riffyn collects data directly from scientific instruments and databases, parses it, and adds experimental context automatically. 


3. VERSION EDITING

Riffyn allows you to re-visit previous versions, and create experiments on any process version

Discover novel correlations

1. AUTOMATED DATA CLEANING

Riffyn’s data-cleaning engine allows you to align data points from different instruments and operators, and summarize data automatically.

2. STATISTICAL ANALYTICS FOR QUALITY

Riffyn's data analytics automatically joins data across steps for multi-factor cause/effect analysis. Riffyn delivers analysis-ready data to the most powerful third-party statistical analytics software including R®, JMP® Statistical Discovery™ software from SAS®, Spotfire®, Tableau® and others.

Collaborate effortlessly

1. SHARING

Any process design and associated experimental data can be shared with view, operate, edit or administrative permissions—much like sharing a Google doc.

2. GETTING DATA OUT (EXPORT/APIS)

Riffyn provides a powerful read/write API to access your data in vendor-neutral format with open-source tools and data standards. You’ll always be able to get all of your data out fully annotated and fully structured.

 
 
 

Parse, contextualize & integrate and analyze all your data in one place

 

Easy in, easy out with your data

GATHER DATA FROM SPREADSHEETS, DATABASES AND MANUAL BATCH RECORDS. SEND IT ANYWHERE FOR ANALYSIS.

Riffyn Bridge collects and parses data from spreadsheets, LIMS, SQL databases, Sharepoint, web data sources, and manual entry. As you collect it, the Riffyn SDE automatically maps that data to the process designs and experimental conditions that produced it—assuring provenance and interpretability for all your data.

Export the structured data with a click, or programmatically via API to any visualization or statistical analysis software. Riffyn offers live data connections and AddIns for many of your favorite tools.
