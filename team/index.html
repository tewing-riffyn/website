Why we're here

We're here for one simple reason, to advance the scientific method into the 21st century with a shifted paradigm—one that recognizes the "source code" of science is the experimental method by which measurements are made. To that end, we are building the Scientific Development Environment that helps write, execute, version control, compute and analyze experimental methods and results—much like a computer engineer would develop, execute and analyze their source code.
